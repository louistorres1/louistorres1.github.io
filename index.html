Lebron James

Lebron James also known as "king james" is a 3 time NBA champion currently playing with the lakers after a recent trade from the Clevland Cavaliers. He is also know for his shocking trade in 2010, when he left the Cavaliers for the Heat.


Background

Born December 30, 1984 in the city of Arkon Ohio. During his early childhood he would move from house to house with his teenage mother Gloria. He showed a great length of talent at an early age as he dominated all the way until High School Where he then was Drafted by the local NBA team "Cleveland Cavaliers."

NBA life

Throughtout his NBA career Lebron has played in a total of 3 teams the: Cleveland Cavaliers, Miami Heat, and Recently the Los Angeles Lakers. The NBA superstar has been recieving a lot of hate throughout his career but also recieves a lot of love from his biggest fans as he continues to inspire many and is considered one the greatest to ever play the game of basketball.
